About your privacy

GloBuy uses Chrome's built-in on-device translation model. Page text is processed entirely within your browser—it never leaves your machine. Only currency codes (like USD, KRW, JPY) are sent to an exchange rate API to fetch the latest rate. Nothing you read, shop for, or type is sent anywhere.
