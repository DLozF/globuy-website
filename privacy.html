Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

The short version

GloBuy translates pages entirely on your device using Chrome's built-in AI. The text you read or type never leaves your browser. Only three-letter currency codes (like USD) are ever sent out — to fetch exchange rates. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

What the extension does with your data

Page content stays on your device

Translation runs entirely on-device using Chrome's built-in Translator and LanguageDetector APIs (Chrome's on-device AI, introduced in Chrome 138). The text of the pages you visit — product names, descriptions, search queries you type — is translated locally inside your browser and is never sent to us or to any third party.

Currency conversion sends only a currency pair

To show converted prices, GloBuy needs the current exchange rate between two currencies. It sends only a pair of three-letter currency codes (for example KRW and USD) to publicly available, keyless exchange-rate APIs:

  • frankfurter.dev / frankfurter.app
  • open.er-api.com (fallback for currencies Frankfurter omits)

No prices, amounts, page content, URLs, or identifiers are included in these requests — just the currency codes. Rates are cached locally for 12 hours to minimize even these requests. They are fetched from the extension's background service worker, not from the pages you visit.

Settings are stored in your browser

Your preferences — target language, target currency, per-site on/off toggle, and glossary and size conversion toggles — are saved with chrome.storage.sync. This is stored in your own browser profile and, if you have Chrome Sync enabled, synced across your own devices by Google. It is never sent to the developer.

Premium translation (coming soon — not active in this version)

A future release will add an optional Premium translation tier for higher-quality translations. It is not present in this version: the extension ships on-device only, there is no Premium toggle, and no page text leaves your device.

When it ships, Premium will be off by default and will only activate after you explicitly turn it on. With Premium on, selected page text would be sent to a translation proxy, forwarded to a third-party LLM provider to produce higher-quality results. Each request would include the text, source/target language, and an anonymous install token (a random ID generated in your browser) used only to meter the free monthly quota — not tied to your identity. URLs you visit, browsing history, and identifiers beyond that token would never be sent. Until that release ships and you opt in, none of this applies.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect, sell, rent, or share any personal information.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, advertising, or fingerprinting.
  • We do not transmit the contents of the pages you browse. This version is on-device only; page text never leaves your device.
  • We do not require an account of any kind.

Permissions

GloBuy requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save your language, currency, and toggle preferences locally.
  • tabs — to read the active tab's URL so the per-site enable/disable toggle works correctly.
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — to inject the translation content script on any page you choose to use GloBuy on. The extension can only be triggered by you (manually or via the per-site toggle); it does not run automatically on every page unless you've enabled that.

Third-party services

The only third-party network requests GloBuy makes are to the exchange-rate APIs listed above. These APIs are public, keyless, and receive only the currency codes described. GloBuy has no affiliation with Frankfurter or Open Exchange Rates.

Children's privacy

GloBuy does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension collects no personal data from any user regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and note it in the extension's changelog. Continued use of the extension after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
